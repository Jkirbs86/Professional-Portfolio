Why Ironman is the greatest avenger


He is very intelligent
He has and IQ of roughly 235. He runs a multi million dollar company with the help of the lovely Pepper Potts. He built his own regenerative life support. He’s built himself, hundreds, if not thousands of Iron Man suits. Some of his suits are even capable of space travel. He’s an expertise in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and computer science. He an electrical and mechanical engineer. He graduated with advanced degrees in physics and engineering at 17 from Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He’s a business man and an economic genius.


He Rises to every challenge
No matter what threat he faces, Tony always seems to be prepared with a new suit and a witty comment to take down whatever means to harm the Earth. He'll do whatever it takes— even if it means putting his life on the line— for the greater good. Even if he fails at first, he will always come back stronger.

He cares.
Although he is well known for being a snarky and sarcastic, he truly cares about the people he’s trying to protect. His entire company is centered around protecting his country. He is a superhero. He wants people to be safe and be able to live their lives out in peace.
